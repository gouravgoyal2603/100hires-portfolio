## My 5 Selection Criteria

**1. Active in 2025–2026**
The expert must have published something relevant — a LinkedIn post, YouTube video, newsletter, or conference talk — within the last 3 months. Stale profiles and people who stopped posting about SEO were excluded regardless of their historical reputation.

**2. Practitioner, not pundit**
The expert must run an actual business, work with real clients, or manage a real website with real traffic consequences. People who only write about SEO without doing it were excluded. I specifically looked for: agency founders, in-house SEO leads at real companies, consultants with documented client work.

**3. AI tools in actual use**
The expert must demonstrate AI in their actual workflow — not just reference AI in passing. This means showing prompts, workflows, scripts, before/after results, or specific tools they use. Thought leaders who say "AI is changing SEO" without showing what they actually do were excluded.

**4. Specific and verifiable**
The expert must share numbers, case studies, or reproducible methods. Vague advice like "create great content" does not qualify. I looked for posts and videos where someone shows: traffic change %, specific prompts used, workflow steps, revenue outcomes.

**5. Not on generic Top-10 listicles**
I deliberately searched for people who do not appear on the first page of "best AI SEO experts 2026" Google results. If someone appears on every mainstream list, they are likely a content marketer, not an active practitioner. The more niche and specific the voice, the more relevant for this research.

## How I Verified Each Expert Was Active in 2025–2026

For each expert, I did the following verification steps:

1. Visited their LinkedIn profile and checked the Posts tab — confirmed at least one post from 2025 or 2026
2. Checked their YouTube channel (where applicable) — confirmed at least one video uploaded in last 6 months
3. Checked their personal site or newsletter for recent publications
4. Cross-referenced their name in recent conference speaker lists or industry roundups from 2025-2026

Any expert whose most recent relevant content was from 2024 or earlier was removed from the list.
