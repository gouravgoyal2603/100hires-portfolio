**Topic:** How LLM answer engines expose their underlying search data providers
**Summary:** Michael shared a technical breakdown showing that GPT-family models reveal underlying search providers (e.g., Bright Data, SerpAPI, Bing) through parameters like "result_source," and that a large share of the products LLMs recommend can be traced directly back to conventional search rank.
