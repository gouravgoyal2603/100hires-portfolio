# Post 2
**Date:** 2026
**LinkedIn URL:** https://ca.linkedin.com/in/rosssimmonds
**Topic:** "Problem core" content strategy for 2026
**Summary:** Ross advocates moving from a "semantic core" keyword approach to a "problem core" approach — listening to what customers actually ask support and complain about, writing content that hits the specific pain point (e.g., "Why You're Overpaying 30% for Cloud and How to Stop It Today" instead of "What is Cloud Services"), and removing jargon customers don't use.
**Key Insight:** In 2026, the winning article isn't the longest one — it's the one that makes the reader think "Finally, someone understands me."
