**Topic:** Building a representative AI search prompt library
**Summary:** Aleyda argues most teams measuring AI search visibility use whatever default or hastily-assembled prompt set a tool provides, producing inaccurate visibility data because the prompts don't reflect real customer journeys.
**Key Insight:** She lays out five principles for a usable prompt library: representative rather than exhaustive, structured rather than random, realistic rather than artificial, repeatable rather than one-off, and mapped to a likely root cause (content gap, source credibility, or entity recognition) whenever a visibility gap shows up.
