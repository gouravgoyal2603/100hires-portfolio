# Post 1
**Date:** Post written Jan 27, 2026; published Jan 30, 2026
**LinkedIn URL:** https://www.seerinteractive.com/insights/do-your-ai-search-seo-kpis-need-a-2026-upgrade-yup *(companion long-form to his LinkedIn post of the same data)*
**Topic:** Whether AI traffic is replacing lost organic search traffic
**Summary:** Wil shares transparent numbers: his organic search traffic dropped by 169,244 visits year over year while AI-generated traffic was only 15,036 visits — meaning AI traffic replaced only about 7.5% of the organic decline, even as revenue held up.
**Key Insight:** He argues teams should stop trying to fully "track" AI's contribution and instead treat quality, trust-building content as a leading indicator, since much of its influence (being shared in private circles, chats, and communities) isn't measurable by conventional analytics.

# Post 2
**Date:** ~2 weeks old (per profile, 2026)
**LinkedIn URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/wilreynolds
**Topic:** GEO's return-on-investment math versus SEO
**Summary:** Wil compares low-risk/slow-tracking brand plays (like sponsorships aimed at influencing AI answers) against classic SEO tactics, and states plainly that Seer's own organic traffic dropped roughly 80% over two years while revenue rose.
**Key Insight:** The math of SEO doesn't transfer cleanly to GEO — trying to apply the same ROI precision that worked for search rankings to AI visibility is, in his words, going to be "a hard pill" for many founders to accept.

# Post 3
**Date:** SEO Week 2026 (session recapped shortly after)
**LinkedIn URL:** https://www.seerinteractive.com/events/on-demand-wil-reynolds-at-seo-week-2026 *(recap of his SEO Week talk, which he also promoted and discussed on LinkedIn)*
**Topic:** "Authentic Intelligence" vs. AI-driven visibility chasing
**Summary:** Wil argues that chasing AI-visibility metrics while ignoring pipeline impact is like "making chicken nuggets and calling yourself a chef" — a short-term win that costs brands long-term trust. He recommends using AI to better understand audiences (e.g., AI-powered interview tools, sales-call transcript analysis) rather than to mass-produce content.
**Key Insight:** Brands can only be believed or chosen if they do the harder, slower work of building real trust — visibility without credibility doesn't convert.
